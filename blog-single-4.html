Patalkot is an unexplored valley featuring a picturesque landscape that lies buried in the lush green covers of vegetation in the Satpura range at an elevation of over 3000 feet. This is the kind of place that can fulfill your craving to explore offbeat places in Madhya Pradesh. The valley is inhabited by only Gonds and Bharias, which are tribal communities.
